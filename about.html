About AI Builders: LatinX Edition

AI Builders: LatinX Edition is a community-driven podcast and initiative that explores the rapidly evolving world of artificial intelligence through the lens of accessibility, education, and cultural representation.

Our mission is twofold:
  1. Research & Education: We break down complex AI topics from neural networks and deep learning to generative AI and AI agents into conversations that are approachable for students, professionals, and lifelong learners. Our research and education efforts will look into tools and platforms like OpenAI to make cutting-edge AI research more understandable, bridging the gap between academic knowledge and real-world application, with a particular emphasis on inclusion for underrepresented voices in technology.
  2. Innovation & Impact: Beyond the podcast, AI Builders: LatinX Edition is building tools, workshops, and community resources that empower the next generation of AI builders. We are exploring prototypes like educational chatbots, accessible AI explainer apps, and community-driven learning platforms. These innovation and impact projects will leverage infrastructure from providers like Microsoft Azure, aligning with our vision of creating scalable solutions that bring AI literacy and practical skills to diverse communities worldwide.

At its core, AI Builders: LatinX Edition is about accessibility, inclusion, and innovation. Whether you are a researcher, developer, entrepreneur, or simply curious about AI, our goal is to create a space where knowledge is shared, creativity is sparked, and opportunities are opened.

THE NAME
AI Builders: LatinX Edition refers to the process by which LatinX voices, stories, and innovations shape the future of AI and technology, highlighting the impact of culture, technology, and people on our world.

We believe that building AI and technology is deeply connected to our lived experiences, cultures, and communities. This podcast is about exploring those connections and how they shape the future.

THE PEOPLE

The AI Builders: LatinX Edition team is made up of passionate technologists, educators, and storytellers. We’re committed to amplifying LatinX voices in AI and tech, and we look forward to bringing in guests and new perspectives as the show grows.
