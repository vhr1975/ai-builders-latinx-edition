Welcome to AI Builders: LatinX Edition, a weekly podcast exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence, technology, and the vibrant voices of the Latinx community. We’re spotlighting the builders, innovators, and changemakers shaping the future of AI while staying rooted in culture, community, and lived experience.

We’re currently testing out our format, technology, and processes, so thank you for bearing with us as we refine the show! Your feedback means the world feel free to connect with us via email or on Twitter to share your thoughts and ideas.
